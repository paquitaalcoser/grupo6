 Questions, yes or no

A yes or no question is a question whose expected answer is one of two options, one that affirms the questions and one that denies the question.
Normally, in English, the options are "yes" or "no".

Question structure

Questions Word + Auxiliary Verb or Modal Auxiliary Verb + Subject + Main Verb

Response structure

Yes / No + subject + Auxiliary verb + Complement or other information

WH-Questions.
Questions that request information of any kind when answering.
Must be accompanied by the auxiliary verb
